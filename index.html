HOT STONE MASSAGE

Enhance SPA Experience

Hot Stone Massage blends therapeutic massage techniques with the added luxury of heated volcanic stones. This treatment is perfect for those who prefer a gentler touch over deep tissue massage, as the warmth of the stones helps ease tension while reducing the need for intense pressure. The heat penetrates deep into the muscles, providing a comforting and effective release of stiffness and soreness.

A 60-minute session is ideal for focusing on the upper body, while extended 90- or 120-minute sessions are available by contacting our clinic directly. Rejuvenate your body, relax your muscles, and restore your energy with this deeply calming massage experience.
